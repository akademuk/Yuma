About TwAc

TwAc is a new generation of skin regeneration boosters, developed with the participation of international biologists and doctors from YUMA Medical LP.

Unique Molecular Organization and PDRN

The technology is based on the molecular structuring of PDRN for the effective activation of cellular processes.

The low-molecular-weight PDRN fragments ensure rapid cellular absorption and uniform distribution in tissues without the formation of nodules or inflammation. They actively stimulate the synthesis of collagen, elastin, and hyaluronic acid (HA), significantly improving skin turgor and the vascular network.

Purity and Enhanced Efficacy

The multi-level PDRN purification system guarantees complete biodegradation without genetic risks and a minimum level of impurities.

Thanks to the stabilized TwAc® matrix, the desired regenerative effect is achieved with a smaller injection volume, making the procedure more comfortable.

Clinically Proven Regeneration

Clinical trials of TwAc have shown a significant increase in CD34-positive vessels up to 173% just 21 days after the procedure, indicating a powerful angiogenic effect.

TwAc is safe, hypoallergenic, fully biodegradable, and meets high international quality standards (ISO 13485).

Maximum Efficacy and Lasting Result

Thanks to PDRN and HA (in 2.0 and 3.0), TwAc provides pronounced regeneration, the results of which are preserved for up to 6-12 months. A high level of defect correction and patient satisfaction is clinically confirmed.

Complete Safety and Biocompatibility

TwAc is a biodegradable, hypoallergenic booster with purified PDRN, a low level of endotoxins, and no toxic impurities or traces in the tissues, which guarantees a high level of safety.

Minimal Traumaticity

TwAc is easily injected with a fine needle; tissue damage is minimal, swelling and redness are reduced, and recovery is fast. Patients note the absence of significant traces of intervention.

Liquid Structure

TwAc is a monophasic booster with optimized PDRN, easily injected through a needle, ensuring uniform distribution, precise positioning, and a natural result without nodules.

Different Concentrations for Different Tasks

Each TwAc has an optimal concentration of PDRN and HA for specific indications. The Eyes, 2.0, and 3.0 line allows choosing the intensity from superficial hydration to deep regeneration.

Confirmed Clinical Efficacy

The unique properties of TwAc are confirmed by clinical studies: effective vascular regeneration, a high level of patient satisfaction, and procedural comfort, which proves the advantages of the preparation in practice.

TwAc Product Line

The TwAc line includes three products — Eyes, 2.0, and 3.0 — all based on PDRN and without lidocaine for maximum biocompatibility. They differ in concentration, viscosity, and indications to cover various zones and skin regeneration needs.

TwAc Eyes

TwAc Eyes is a delicate booster created for the regeneration of the periorbital zone, improving the quality of the skin in the lower third of the face, and treating alopecia. The preparation is ideally suited for thin, sensitive skin where a mild effect without excessive volume is needed.

Indications:

Superficial wrinkles around the eyes, dark circles, puffiness, skin atony (loss of tone) in the lower third of the face, alopecia.

Injection depth:

Subdermal, 1–4 mm — directly under the wrinkle or into the area of puffiness, which reduces the risk of lumps forming and ensures a uniform effect.

Composition:

PDRN $10 \text{ mg/ml}$. The concentration is selected for superficial skin layers — it stimulates cellular regeneration while remaining light and malleable in the tissues.

Result:

Post-course, the eye area appears more elastic, brighter, and smoother, with reduced wrinkles and dark circles lasting up to 3 months.

Course:

3 procedures with an interval of 7 days.

Features:

Low-molecular solution with low viscosity, easily spreads in the superficial layers, does not create indurations (firming/nodules), and ensures comfort during administration.

Volume:

1 ml, needle 34G.

Phillex2.0

Phillex2.0 is a universal medium-intensity booster created for moisturizing, skin brightening, and anti-aging prophylaxis. The preparation is ideally suited for patients with combined aging zones who require a moderate effect without excessive volume.

Indications:

Skin dryness, fine wrinkles, uneven tone, skin atony (loss of tone) of the face, neck, décolleté, and hands.

Injection depth:

Subdermal 2-3 mm — using the papule or linear-retrograde technique into the dermis, which ensures uniform moisturizing and stimulation of regeneration.

Composition:

PDRN 7.5 mg/ml + HA 12 mg/ml. The concentration is selected for the middle layers of the skin — it maintains turgor and radiance, while remaining plastic in the tissues.

Result:

Post-treatment, skin appears elastic, smooth, and radiant, with fine wrinkles diminished and a natural, rested look lasting up to 3 months.

Course:

2 procedures with an interval of 14 days.

Features:

The medium viscosity of the solution allows the preparation to be uniformly distributed in the tissues, preventing indurations (nodules) and ensuring comfort during administration.

Volume:

3 ml, needle 30G.

TwAc 3.0

TwAc 3.0 is a high-intensity booster designed for deep regeneration and tissue restoration. The preparation is ideally suited for areas requiring pronounced lifting and the formation of a skin framework without excessive volume.

Indications:

Deep wrinkles, atony, decreased turgor of the face, neck, décolleté, hands, and body.

Injection depth:

Subdermal 3-5 mm — at the border of the dermis and subcutaneous fat, which provides pronounced lifting and long-lasting regeneration.

Composition:

PDRN 20 mg/ml + HA 20 mg/ml. The highest concentration among the three products provides a shape-retaining effect, remaining homogeneous and not creating nodules upon injection.

Result:

After the course, the skin becomes denser, firmer, and smoother. Deep wrinkles are smoothed, tone is evened out, pigmentation is lightened, and the face looks rested and natural. The effect lasts up to 3 months.

Course:

2 procedures with an interval of 21 days.

Features:

The highly concentrated solution forms an internal framework, stimulates neocollagenesis, and supports tissues, preventing the formation of indurations and ensuring comfort during administration.

Volume:

3 ml needle 27G.

Enhanced Comfort & Efficacy: The Phillex Advantage

It contains 0.3% lidocaine in its composition, which nullifies pain during the injection procedure. In turn, the laser-cut needle minimizes discomfort at the moment of injection.

To investigate the patient's comfort level, two preparations were tested: Phillex Deep (without lidocaine) and Phillex Deep Plus (with lidocaine). The study demonstrated identical efficacy with no change in product quality, while showing increased patient comfort during the procedure in the group of preparations containing lidocaine.

Clinician Comfort

When developing Phillex, Yuma Medical focused not only on patient comfort but also on clinician ease and confidence. Every detail – from the syringe to the gel’s consistency – is designed to make injections controlled, smooth, and precise.

Ergonomic
Phillex Syringe

Phillex is supplied in modern syringes specifically designed for aesthetic practitioners. Wide finger “wings” provide a secure grip, while extended rests prevent slipping, reduce hand fatigue during multiple injections, and give better control over injection depth and speed. The plunger moves smoothly without jerks, thanks to precise fitting and special lubrication, ensuring even and controlled gel delivery. Clear graduated markings allow tracking the injected volume with 0.01 ml accuracy, helping maintain symmetry and follow injection protocols. All syringes and needles are sterile and meet medical standards, giving clinicians confidence and safety in every procedure.

Reliability and quality
in every syringe

Phillex is produced with strict quality control. Syringes are made from medical-grade bioinert plastic, and needles come individually capped with sterility markings to ensure sharpness and integrity. All accessories meet European certification standards. For clinicians, this means consistent gel texture, smooth plunger operation, and durable needles, providing confidence, comfort, and predictable results for both practitioner and patient.

Ease and control
of injection

The thixotropic gel and improved instruments make Phillex technically simple to use. The filler flows easily through the needle with minimal pressure, allowing one-handed operation. Laser-sharpened needles create almost imperceptible skin entry, while the gel passes smoothly through dense or fibrous tissue. This lets the clinician focus fully on technique and shaping, ensuring precise, predictable results, while patient comfort is maintained by lidocaine.
