About TwAc

TwAc is a new generation of skin regeneration boosters, developed with the participation of international biologists and doctors from YUMA Medical LP.

Unique Molecular Organization and PDRN

The technology is based on the molecular structuring of PDRN for the effective activation of cellular processes.

The low-molecular-weight PDRN fragments ensure rapid cellular absorption and uniform distribution in tissues without the formation of nodules or inflammation. They actively stimulate the synthesis of collagen, elastin, and hyaluronic acid (HA), significantly improving skin turgor and the vascular network.

Purity and Enhanced Efficacy

The multi-level PDRN purification system guarantees complete biodegradation without genetic risks and a minimum level of impurities.

Thanks to the stabilized TwAc® matrix, the desired regenerative effect is achieved with a smaller injection volume, making the procedure more comfortable.

Clinically Proven Regeneration

Clinical trials of TwAc have shown a significant increase in CD34-positive vessels up to 173% just 21 days after the procedure, indicating a powerful angiogenic effect.

TwAc is safe, hypoallergenic, fully biodegradable, and meets high international quality standards (ISO 13485).

Proven efficacy supported by research

TwAc has a strong evidence base: histological studies assessing CD34-positive vessels, along with clinical observations and VAS questionnaires, confirm tissue regeneration, improved skin quality, and high patient satisfaction.

PDRN with ultra-high molecular weight ≥1,480 kDa

The PDRN in the TwAc formulation has an ultra-high molecular weight of at least 1,480 kDa, placing it among the highest-molecular-weight PDRN products currently available on the market. This elevated molecular weight is considered particularly effective for regenerative processes, ensures pronounced biological activity, and supports intensive tissue repair and skin regeneration.

Prolonged effect thanks to hyaluronic acid and PDRN

In TwAc 2.0 and TwAc 3.0, hyaluronic acid forms a reservoir-like matrix that enables gradual PDRN release into tissues, providing a prolonged effect with lasting hydration, turgor, radiance, and visible results even after fewer treatment sessions.

High safety and quality profile

Salmon-derived PDRN in TwAc undergoes multistep purification, has low endotoxin levels, and is fully biodegradable. ISO 13485–certified manufacturing and full biocompatibility testing minimise the risk of inflammation, allergic reactions, and long-term complications.

TWAc product line: a differentiated approach to regenerative therapy

The TwAc range (TWAc Eyes, TWAc 2.0 and TWAc 3.0) has been developed as an integrated system of regenerative boosters that covers the full spectrum of clinical indications in aesthetic dermatology and regenerative medicine.

Comprehensive portfolio for different clinical needs

TwAc Eyes, TwAc 2.0 and TwAc 3.0 cover a full range of clinical indications—from periorbital care and preventive revitalisation to deep regeneration and lifting—enabling personalised, coordinated treatment protocols.

TwAc Product Line

The TwAc line includes three products — Eyes, 2.0, and 3.0 — all based on PDRN and without lidocaine for maximum biocompatibility. They differ in concentration, viscosity, and indications to cover various zones and skin regeneration needs.

TwAc Eyes

TwAc Eyes is a delicate booster created for the regeneration of the periorbital zone, improving the quality of the skin in the lower third of the face, and treating alopecia. The preparation is ideally suited for thin, sensitive skin where a mild effect without excessive volume is needed.

Indications:

Superficial wrinkles around the eyes, dark circles, puffiness, skin atony (loss of tone) in the lower third of the face, alopecia.

Injection depth:

Subdermal, 1–4 mm — directly under the wrinkle or into the area of puffiness, which reduces the risk of lumps forming and ensures a uniform effect.

Composition:

PDRN $10 \text{ mg/ml}$. The concentration is selected for superficial skin layers — it stimulates cellular regeneration while remaining light and malleable in the tissues.

Result:

Post-course, the eye area appears more elastic, brighter, and smoother, with reduced wrinkles and dark circles lasting up to 3 months.

Course:

3 procedures with an interval of 7 days.

Features:

Low-molecular solution with low viscosity, easily spreads in the superficial layers, does not create indurations (firming/nodules), and ensures comfort during administration.

Volume:

1 ml, needle 34G.

TwAc 2.0

TwAc 2.0 is a universal medium-intensity booster designed to hydrate, brighten the skin, and prevent aging. The product is ideal for patients with combined aging zones where a moderate effect is needed without excessive volume.

Indications:

Skin dryness, fine wrinkles, uneven tone, skin atony (loss of tone) of the face, neck, décolleté, and hands.

Injection depth:

Subdermal 2-3 mm — using the papule or linear-retrograde technique into the dermis, which ensures uniform moisturizing and stimulation of regeneration.

Composition:

PDRN 7.5 mg/ml + HA 12 mg/ml. The concentration is selected for the middle layers of the skin — it maintains turgor and radiance, while remaining plastic in the tissues.

Result:

Post-treatment, skin appears elastic, smooth, and radiant, with fine wrinkles diminished and a natural, rested look lasting up to 3 months.

Course:

2 procedures with an interval of 14 days.

Features:

The medium viscosity of the solution allows the preparation to be uniformly distributed in the tissues, preventing indurations (nodules) and ensuring comfort during administration.

Volume:

3 ml, needle 30G.

TwAc 3.0

TwAc 3.0 is a high-intensity booster designed for deep regeneration and tissue restoration. The preparation is ideally suited for areas requiring pronounced lifting and the formation of a skin framework without excessive volume.

Indications:

Deep wrinkles, atony, decreased turgor of the face, neck, décolleté, hands, and body.

Injection depth:

Subdermal 3-5 mm — at the border of the dermis and subcutaneous fat, which provides pronounced lifting and long-lasting regeneration.

Composition:

PDRN 20 mg/ml + HA 20 mg/ml. The highest concentration among the three products provides a shape-retaining effect, remaining homogeneous and not creating nodules upon injection.

Result:

After the course, the skin becomes denser, firmer, and smoother. Deep wrinkles are smoothed, tone is evened out, pigmentation is lightened, and the face looks rested and natural. The effect lasts up to 3 months.

Course:

2 procedures with an interval of 21 days.

Features:

The highly concentrated solution forms an internal framework, stimulates neocollagenesis, and supports tissues, preventing the formation of indurations and ensuring comfort during administration.

Volume:

3 ml needle 27G.

Delicacy and Periorbital Zone Restoration

TwAc Eyes is applied to the periorbital zone, the lower third of the face, and the scalp. It provides pronounced hydration, reduces fine wrinkles (crow's feet), brightens dark circles, and stimulates local angiogenesis, yielding the best results when introduced superficially while minimizing the risk of swelling.

Periorbital Zone: Delicate Revitalization

TwAc Eyes, with its low-concentration PDRN structure, hydrates the delicate skin around the eyes, reduces fine lines, and brightens dark circles for a smooth, low-risk result.

Lower Third of the Face: Refining Contours and Texture

TwAc Eyes targets early signs of sagging and fine lines in the lower face, offering hydration and bio-revitalization to enhance skin texture and provide subtle support for a refreshed look.

Scalp Revitalization: Stimulating Growth

TwAc Eyes promotes scalp health by enhancing circulation and revitalizing dormant hair follicles. Its gentle formula ensures comfort while supporting denser, healthier hair growth.

TwAc 2.0

Hydration, Tone, and Radiance

TwAc 2.0 is recommended for the face, neck, décolleté, and hands. It is ideal for patients with moderate atony and initial signs of photo- and chrono-aging. The product intensely hydrates, brightens, evens out skin tone, and maintains skin firmness. Thanks to its medium PDRN concentration, TwAc 2.0 effectively stimulates fibroblasts for collagen and elastin synthesis, providing a noticeable revitalization effect and reducing superficial pigmentation.

Facial Revitalization: Tone & Radiance

TwAc 2.0 hydrates and revitalizes the face, combating moderate atony and early aging signs. It brightens the complexion, restores tone, and maintains firmness for a radiant look.

Neck Contouring: Firmness and Hydration

The neck's delicate skin needs special care. TwAc® 2.0 hydrates and boosts collagen, enhancing firmness and elasticity while preserving a youthful contour.

Décolleté Brightening: Hydration and Uniform Tone

The décolleté is susceptible to sun damage. TwAc 2.0 hydrates, brightens, and evens skin tone, reducing pigmentation and smoothing texture for a radiant chest.

Hand Rejuvenation: Volume and Elasticity

Hands are prone to photodamage and volume loss. TwAc 2.0 hydrates and improves skin tone, enhancing elasticity and reducing the appearance of veins for a youthful look.

TwAc 3.0

Deep Regeneration and Lifting

TwAc 3.0 is used for the face, neck, décolleté, hands, and body. This is the most potent booster in the line, intended for deep regeneration, intensive lifting, and the restoration of turgor in cases of significant loss of elasticity. Its high PDRN concentration ensures prolonged stimulation of collagenogenesis and the restoration of the dermal structural components. It is used for the correction of scars, stretch marks (striae), and skin recovery after aggressive procedures.

Facial Intensive Lifting: Deep Regeneration

For maximum support and restoration, TwAc 3.0 is ideal. Its high PDRN concentration promotes deep regeneration, collagen production, and restores firmness and volume for rejuvenation.

Neck Tightening: Turgor and Deep Structure

The neck shows early signs of aging. TwAc 3.0 provides deep regeneration and lifting, restoring elasticity, reducing "tech neck" lines, and enhancing neck contour.

Décolleté Restoration: Repairing Sun Damage

This treatment targets severe structural damage and crepiness in the décolleté, using TwAc 3.0 for deep regeneration, restoring skin thickness, elasticity, and minimizing deep lines.

Hand Renewal: Structural Restoration
