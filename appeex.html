About APPeex peleeng

This 3-in-1 formula redefines peeling. While TCA smooths skin texture, PDRN penetrates deep to repair DNA and boost collagen. Cyanocobalamin soothes and protects, restoring natural radiance immediately after treatment.

The Synergy of Exfoliation and Repair

APPeex is a new generation exfoliant with pronounced regenerating properties. Its unique formula combines TCA 20% + PDRN 5% + Vitamin B12, which not only ensures uniform exfoliation but also triggers regenerative processes and reduces post-procedure erythema.

Phase I — Exfoliation (TCA)

Stabilized trichloroacetic acid denatures proteins of the stratum corneum → ensuring rapid and uniform exfoliation.

Phase II — Inactivation (B12)

Vitamin B12 inactivates nitric oxide and reduces vasodilation → providing redness control.

Phase III — Regeneration (PDRN):

Polynucleotides stimulate repair, angiogenesis, and cell proliferation.

APPeex Peeling

A next-generation professional chemical peel with pronounced regenerating action. This unique system is designed to effectively renew the skin surface while simultaneously stimulating internal recovery processes.

Composition:

TCA 20%, PDRN 5%, Vitamin B12 + auxiliary components (propylene glycol, sodium hydroxide, glycerin, urea, amino acids, etc.).

Mechanism:

TCA promotes quick exfoliation, PDRN boosts collagen and GAG production, and B12 regulates vasodilation, reducing post-procedure erythema.

Indications:

Photo- and chrono-aging, hyperpigmentation, uneven microrelief (skin texture), post-acne marks, scars, striae (stretch marks), enlarged pores.

Course:

4–6 procedures with an interval of 7–10 days.
