About YUMA

Innovation and Safety

YUMA MEDICAL LP is a UK-based company that develops and manufactures medical devices and products for aesthetic medicine. We combine science, modern technologies, and high safety standards to help doctors effectively address age-related skin changes.

All operations are regulated by the ISO 13485 standard, and every stage—from design to post-marketing surveillance—meets international requirements.

Experts in Skin Health and Beauty

Together with partner manufacturing facilities, the company employs around 2,000 specialists, including engineers, scientists, technologists, and managers, who create innovative and safe solutions for maintaining the beauty and health of the skin.

TwAc: Next Generation Skin Regeneration Boosters

TwAc is a new generation of skin regeneration boosters, developed with the participation of international biologists and physicians from YUMA Medical LP. The core of its technology involves the molecular structuring of PDRN to activate cellular processes.

TwAc is safe, hypoallergenic, fully biodegradable, and complies with international quality standards (ISO 13485).

Learn More

APPeex: Regenerative Exfoliant of the New Generation

APPeex is a new-generation exfoliant with pronounced regenerative properties. Its unique formula combines TCA 20% + PDRN 5% + Vitamin B12.

Thanks to this composition, the peel not only provides uniform exfoliation but also triggers regenerative processes and reduces the severity of post-procedure erythema (redness).

Learn More

Fortox: Botulinum Toxin Type A for Precision

Fortox is an advanced preparation based on Botulinum Toxin Type A from Clostridium botulinum, developed by YUMA MEDICAL LP for aesthetic and therapeutic correction.

Built upon the latest manufacturing technologies, FORTOX ensures unmatched toxin purity at 99.8%, positioning it as a leader among analogs.

This product is created for practitioners who seek maximum efficacy, predictable results, and patient satisfaction.

Our Journey of Innovation

Yuma Medical LP Timeline:

2016
Yuma Medical LP was founded and the Phillex product line was launched

YUMA MEDICAL LP was founded in the UK by PhD Dr. Yudin M. After gaining many years of practical experience in the medical devices field and noticing a lack of high-quality products on the market, the company focused on developing hyaluronic acid–based fillers. This led to the creation of the PHILLEX product line.

2017
PDRN research and the introduction of TwAc

A new stage begins — the research and development of products based on polydeoxyribonucleotides (PDRN), one of the most promising anti-aging molecules with high regenerative properties. The result of this work is the TwAc® product.

2018
Partnerships and the start of TwAc / Phillex research

YUMA MEDICAL LP selects partners for contract manufacturing and launches preclinical studies. The production of TwAc / PHILLEX is organized in accordance with international quality and safety standards.

2020
Successful international product launch

The company successfully commercializes its products. YUMA MEDICAL LP products enter the markets of various countries and receive high recognition from aesthetic medicine practitioners.

2021
Launch of the innovative APPeex product line

YUMA MEDICAL LP actively invests in the development of peels based on TCA and PDRN. This leads to the launch of the APPeex system — an all-season, next-generation three-phase peel with a unique patented combination of TCA 20%, PDRN 5%, and cyanocobalamin (vitamin B12).

Along with the peel, APPeex Cream is introduced to the market — a post-peel cream containing PDRN and moisturizing components. It is used as the final step of the procedure and as a home-care product between sessions, enhancing and prolonging the results of APPeex therapy.

The launch of the APPeex line demonstrates a sevenfold increase in demand within its category.

2023
Partnership between Yuma Medical LP and cMed Italy to develop APPeex

The APPeex peel continues to evolve in partnership with cMed Italy, combining the British scientific approach with Italian expertise in aesthetic medicine.

2025
Line extension: launch of APPeex Strong

The line is expanded with APPeex Strong — a product with an increased TCA concentration of 35%, designed for more intensive protocols, including treatments not only for the face but also for the body.

Today, YUMA MEDICAL LP remains a dynamic R&D company that consistently expands its portfolio of solutions to address involutional skin changes and improve patients’ quality of life.
